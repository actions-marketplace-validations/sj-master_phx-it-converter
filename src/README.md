# PHX-IT Converter

**Free, client-side image to icon converter — no uploads, 100% private.**

Convert any image into ICO, PNG, favicons, Apple touch icons, Android app icons, and more. Everything runs in your browser — your images never leave your device.

---

## Features

- **ICO Conversion** — Generate `.ico` files at 16×16 through 256×256
- **Favicon Ready** — One-click favicon packs for websites
- **Apple Touch Icons** — All standard sizes from 57×57 to 180×180
- **Android Icons** — 192×192 and 512×512 for Play Store
- **PNG, JPG, WebP & BMP** — Export in any common format
- **Bulk ZIP Download** — Select multiple formats and download all at once
- **Drag & Drop** — Just drag an image onto the page
- **Live Preview** — Click any converted icon to see it full-size before downloading
- **Zero Uploads** — 100% client-side processing via HTML5 Canvas
- **Free Forever** — No accounts, no limits, no paywalls

---

## How It Works

1. **Drop an image** onto the converter area (or click to browse)
2. **Select output formats** — pick any combination of ICO, PNG, Apple, Android, JPG, WebP, or BMP
3. **Convert** — processing happens instantly in your browser
4. **Preview** — click any thumbnail to see a larger preview
5. **Download** — grab individual files or a ZIP of everything

No sign-up. No uploads. No server-side processing. Your images stay on your device.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18 + Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion
- **Image Processing**: HTML5 Canvas API + custom ICO binary encoder
- **ZIP Generation**: Custom store-method ZIP builder (no dependencies)
- **Platform**: Base44

---

## Privacy

PHX-IT Converter processes **everything in your browser**. Images are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, or have access to your files. See [Privacy Policy](https://phx-it.com/privacy) for full details.
